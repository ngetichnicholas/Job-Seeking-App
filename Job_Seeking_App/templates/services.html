Our Services

Flex-Connectors offers great services to both job seekers and employers.

At Your Service

Post Job Seeker profiles

Job seekers sign up and log in to the system. Once the job seeker is authenticated, he/she can update profile and upload documents and portfolio links.

Allow Employers to browse profiles

Employers needs to sign up, login and update their profiles. Then after the employer makes payment via mpesa to be allowed to browse job seekers profiles and contact them.

Search jobseekers

Employers can search job seekers matching their job category and see their profiles and resumes after which they can contact them.

Connect Jobseeker and Employer

This system provides a platform where employers can reach to prospective job seekers anytime and conviniently. The details of job seekers are availble for easy reach for job offer.
